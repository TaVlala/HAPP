Interactions & Rules

The rules that must not be broken — timing and combinations that affect safety or efficacy

⛔ Must not break
⛔
Zinc (22mg) + Selenium (100mcg) — never at the same time
Compete for the same absorption pathway. Zinc with lunch, Selenium with breakfast. Minimum 2h+ apart.
⛔
L-Theanine + Corsis (phenobarbital) — skip L-theanine on Corsis days
Both are CNS-calming agents. Do not stack two sedatives on the same day — unpredictable combined effect.
⛔
Psyllium husk + any medication — 1–2h separation mandatory
Psyllium physically binds to medications and dramatically reduces their absorption. Non-negotiable.
⛔
Oral NSAIDs (ibuprofen) without gastroprotection
Caused current flare. Always: paracetamol first. Topical diclofenac gel second. Oral NSAID last resort only — always with food + PPI + minimum dose + minimum duration.
⛔
Probiotics + Nexium — take at least 2h after Nexium dose
Acid suppression reduces but doesn't eliminate survival risk for bacteria. Evening timing is optimal for overnight colonisation.
⚠️ Be careful
⚠️
Curcumin + Corsis — discuss with doctor before adding
Possible CYP450 interaction. Since Corsis is only occasional, risk is low. Raise it at your next appointment.
⚠️
Nexium impairs absorption of Zinc, B12, Magnesium
Space all three at least 2h from each Nexium dose. Less critical on short course but good habit.
⚠️
Zinc 22mg + Zinc-Carnosine 75mg — separate meals
Different compounds, not duplicates. But avoid same meal to prevent excessive zinc load. Zinc at lunch, zinc-carnosine with lunch AND dinner.
⚠️
Fat-soluble supplements without dietary fat = poor absorption
D3, K2, Omega-3, Quercetin, Phosphatidylserine all need fat at the same meal. Even 1 tsp olive oil or 1 egg is sufficient.
⚠️
Methylfolate — watch for overstimulation
Some people with anxiety are sensitive to high folate. If you notice increased anxiety after starting, reduce to 400mcg and reassess.
✅ Beneficial combinations
✅
Vitamin D3 + K2 MK-7 — always take together
K2 directs calcium mobilised by D3 to bones, not soft tissue. Mandatory pair at D3 doses above 1000 IU.
✅
Methylcobalamin B12 + Methylfolate — take as a pair
Both work in the methylation cycle. Taking together is correct and synergistic. Morning with breakfast.
✅
Magnesium glycinate + L-Theanine at bedtime — ideal stack
Complementary pathways for sleep and anxiety. No interaction concerns. Best natural sleep/anxiety combination.
✅
Collagen peptides + Vitamin C source
Vitamin C is a cofactor in collagen synthesis. Squeeze of lemon in water alongside collagen, or any vitamin C food at breakfast.
